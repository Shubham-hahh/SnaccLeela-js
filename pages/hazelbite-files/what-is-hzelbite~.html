What Is Hazelbite?~

The posts are mostly written by me but handed to LLMs for grammar corrections, paragraph fixes or just making my vast thoughts readable in a wide scale. Being trans is already a struggle of explaining yourself to other people or figuring yourself out through the complex struggles you face as a women in a different body.
